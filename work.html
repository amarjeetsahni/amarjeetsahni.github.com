Our Work, Contributions And Goals

Establishment of free training centres for weaker sections of the society and for the persons with disability.
Establishment of free educational institutions for children of underprivileged class and illiterate adults.
To create awareness about various government programs, give the necessary information and knowledge to the respected target groups to enable them to get benefit of the schemes.
To create awareness in the society for eradication of social evils.
To help poor talented students so that poverty is not an impediment in their life.
To arrange for free legal assistance to the people who can not otherwise afford it.
